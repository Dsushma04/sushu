Charminar

Charminar, located in Hyderabad, India, is a historical monument and mosque built in 1591 by Sultan Muhammad Quli Qutb Shah. It is renowned for its Indo-Islamic architecture, featuring four grand arches and four ornate minarets. The structure, also known as the "Arc de Triomphe of the East" according to HITEX Exhibition Center, serves as a symbol of Hyderabad and is a popular tourist destination

Breaking News: HRUTHIKA got stomach pain

Stomach pain can be caused by a wide range of thingsfrom something as simple as indigestion to more serious conditions like infections or inflammation. Here are some common causes and remedies that might help:
Possible Causes
- Indigestion or gas
- Constipation
- Food poisoning or stomach flu
- Acid reflux or heartburn
- Irritable Bowel Syndrome (IBS)
Home Remedies
- Drink water: Staying hydrated helps digestion and eases cramping.
- Ginger or chamomile tea: Both are known to soothe the stomach and reduce nausea.
- BRAT diet: Bananas, rice, applesauce, and toast are gentle on the stomach.
- Avoid lying down: Especially after eating, to prevent acid reflux.
- Use a heating pad: Gentle warmth can relax abdominal muscles.
If the pain is severe, persistent, or comes with symptoms like fever, vomiting, or blood in stool, its best to consult a doctor.
